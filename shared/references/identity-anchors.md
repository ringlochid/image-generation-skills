# Subject identity anchors

Use when a generated asset must preserve a subject across variants.

- Character/person: face shape, hair, eyes, outfit, palette, accessories, silhouette, required motifs, forbidden drift.
- Animal/pet: species/breed, body shape, markings, ears/tail, collar/accessories, palette, expression range.
- Mascot/object/product: silhouette, proportions, material, color blocking, distinctive parts, logo/text handling, forbidden substitutions.
- Brand mark: primary geometry, negative space, palette, stroke/edge style, scalability, forbidden motifs.

Do not force human fields onto non-human subjects. Pick only the anchors that make the subject recognizable.
