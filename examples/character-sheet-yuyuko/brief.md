# Character brief
Yuyuko Saigyouji is an elegant ghost princess from Touhou Project, adapted here into a reusable anime character reference sheet. The visual premise is soft, ethereal, playful elegance: a pale pink-haired girl in a blue-and-white frilled outfit with a distinctive blue mob cap/bonnet, red ghost-swirl insignia, folding fan, cherry blossom motifs, and gentle supernatural wisps.

## Key visual elements
- Short/wavy pale pink hair, soft bangs, rounded anime face.
- Blue-and-white frilled kimono-dress / ghost-princess outfit with dark blue bow and trim.
- Large blue mob cap/bonnet with frilled edge and a red ghost-swirl insignia on the front panel.
- Purple patterned folding fan as the main hand prop.
- Cherry blossom petals and small ghostly flame/wisp motifs as secondary effects.
- Soft blue, white, pale pink, purple, and red accent palette.

## Pose and expression notes
- Elegant, relaxed posture; refined but playful.
- Expressions should include serene smile, fan-hidden smug smile, surprised, sleepy, crying, excited, and pouting.
- Use fan and sleeves to create graceful silhouettes.

## Outfit and accessories
- Preserve the bonnet shape and red ghost-swirl insignia clearly.
- Preserve frilled sleeves, blue bow, layered white ruffles, and blue dress/kimono silhouette.
- Folding fan can be open or closed but should remain purple/patterned.

## Palette and materials
- Main colors: soft powder blue, white, pale pink hair, dark blue ribbon accents.
- Accent colors: red insignia, purple fan, pink cherry blossoms, pale blue ghost wisps.
- Materials feel soft cloth, frills, satin ribbon, paper fan.

## Consistency rules / forbidden drift
- Do not add animal ears, swords, horns, armor, modern streetwear, or unrelated props.
- Do not change the bonnet into a witch hat or remove the red ghost-swirl insignia.
- Do not use readable labels/text inside generated art.
