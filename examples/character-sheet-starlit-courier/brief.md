# Character brief — Nami, Starlit Courier

Nami is an adult aerial courier who delivers sealed letters between rooftop observatories in a coastal city. She is nimble, optimistic, and alert, with quick messenger body language and a practical sense of style. Her design blends urban delivery gear with soft celestial navigation motifs: crescent clasps, star-map lining, wind ribbons, and a compact telescope case.

## Key visual elements
- Adult woman with an athletic, light-footed silhouette and bright messenger energy.
- Short midnight-blue hair with a wind-swept bob and one pale silver streak.
- Clear gray-blue eyes, lively focused expression, small confident smile.
- Cropped ivory flight jacket over slate-blue tunic, high-waisted dark shorts over leggings, practical ankle boots.
- Crescent clasp, small satchel, sealed letter bundle, compact brass telescope case, wind-ribbon scarf.

## Pose and expression notes
- Main pose should feel ready to leap across rooftops, but not extreme action.
- Expressions: alert neutral, bright grin, focused squint, surprised laugh.
- Body language is quick, helpful, and trustworthy.

## Outfit and accessories
- Jacket is practical, slightly oversized, with clean seams and rolled cuffs.
- Satchel and letter bundle are required identity props.
- Telescope case and crescent clasp are signature accessories.
- Wind-ribbon scarf can trail slightly but should not look like wings.

## Palette and materials
- Palette: ivory, slate blue, midnight navy, brass gold, pale silver, soft sky-cyan accent.
- Materials: canvas jacket, leather satchel/boots, brass hardware, soft fabric scarf.

## Consistency rules / forbidden drift
- No animal ears, no halo, no wings, no school uniform, no heavy armor, no readable text.
- Do not turn the courier theme into a magical girl outfit.
- Keep the design practical and messenger-like across all panels.
